Lack of an easy and efficient text input mechanism in Indic scripts has long remained barrier in flow of Information & Communication Technology (ICT) depriving the Non-English speaking population void of potential opportunities with various ICTs available. Swarachakra is a novel attempt by team of designers,technocrats and language enthusiasts to help people communicate without the English bottleneck. With this project, we not only aim to facilitate people to use technology without a language barrier but in efficient and lucid ways.
The project originated when Anirudha Joshi focused on the aspect of people facing difficulty in text entry and took the challenge upon himself. Later the project grew in itself and today, a multi-disciplinary team works towards a much less focused cause of social empowerment because the next million smartphone are going to be in pockets of Indians who might not be as English friendly as their own language.
